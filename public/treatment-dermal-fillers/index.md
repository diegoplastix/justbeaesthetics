## Where Lip Filler Can Help

Lip filler at JustBE is used to refine, define, and balance. Every result is personalized by Dr. Marchuk based on your lip anatomy, your facial proportions, and what you are actually trying to achieve. No templates. No default approach. 

### Volume Enhancement

For lips that are naturally thin or have lost volume with age, filler adds soft, natural fullness without the overdone appearance that makes results obvious. 

### Lip Definition

A precise approach to sharpening the border of the lips, creating cleaner definition and a more polished appearance without adding visible volume. 

### Symmetry Correction

Many people have naturally uneven lips. Targeted filler placement addresses asymmetry and brings better balance between the upper and lower lip. 

### Cupid's Bow Definition

A subtle technique to accentuate the natural shape of the upper lip, restoring or enhancing the curve that often softens with age or sits undefined naturally. 

### Vertical Lip Lines

Fine lines around the mouth that appear with age can be softened using precise filler placement, reducing the shadow and texture that make lips look older. 

### Overall Proportion

Sometimes it is less about volume and more about how the lips relate to the rest of the face. Dr. Marchuk evaluates proportion holistically before any filler is placed. 

Treatment always begins with a consultation. Dr. Marchuk evaluates your lip anatomy, your facial structure, and your goals before recommending any filler or any amount. No rushed appointments. No defaulting to the most popular technique. Just thoughtful, physician-led care designed to look entirely like you.

## Frequently Asked Questions

What makes JustBE different from a typical med spa? 

At JustBE, every treatment is administered by Dr. Marchuk directly. No nurses, no rotating injectors, no volume-driven appointments. Lip filler here is one part of a broader picture of your health, not a standalone service sold by the syringe. 

Will lip filler make my lips look overdone? 

Not with the approach taken here. The goal is a result that looks like your lips, just better defined or slightly fuller. Dr. Marchuk starts conservatively and builds from there, which is the safest way to achieve natural results. 

How long does lip filler last? 

Most clients see results for six to twelve months. Factors like metabolism, product used, and how your body processes hyaluronic acid all influence duration. 

Is there any downtime after lip filler? 

Mild swelling and tenderness are normal for 24 to 72 hours after treatment. Most clients are comfortable returning to their day immediately. Final results are visible once swelling resolves, typically within one to two weeks. 

Do I need to be part of a wellness program to get lip filler? 

No. Lip filler is available as a standalone service. That said, Dr. Marchuk may ask questions about your overall health during your consultation because understanding the full picture helps her make better treatment decisions.
