From Nutritional Science to Precision Aesthetics

Dr. Adir Marchuk began her career in nutrition, not aesthetics. That distinction matters more than you might think.

She founded JustBE Aesthetics on the premise that genuine wellness cannot be separated from how we look and feel. She had seen too many clients cycle through treatments that addressed the surface but ignored the root. Weight loss programs that ignored hormones. Aesthetic clinics that ignored skin health. Practitioners who treated symptoms without ever asking: why is this happening in the first place?

Her answer was to build something different. A practice where blood type nutrition, metabolic analysis, hormonal balance, and lifestyle coaching come first, and where aesthetic treatments like Botox and lip filler serve as the finishing touch on a body that is already working well from the inside.
