##### FAQ

## Answers to Common Questions

Telehealth & Logistics

What is the difference between what Dr. Marchuk does and a standard nutrition program? 

Most nutrition programs give you a plan built around general guidelines. Dr. Marchuk builds around your specific biology, using blood type nutrition principles, CBC panels, and food allergen testing to identify what your body actually needs. Two people can follow the same plan and have completely different outcomes. Understanding your individual physiology is what makes the difference. 

Do I need to come in for lab work before starting? 

Lab work is a core part of how the program works. A CBC panel and food allergen testing give Dr. Marchuk the data she needs to make informed recommendations rather than educated guesses. In most cases labs can be ordered following your initial consultation and reviewed together before your plan is finalized. 

What does a nutrition program at JustBE actually include? 

It starts with a deep-dive consultation covering your health history, lifestyle, current symptoms, and goals. From there, Dr. Marchuk develops a blood type nutrition protocol, reviews your lab results, and builds a personalized plan that addresses food, sleep, hormones, stress, and exercise together. Ongoing coaching and adjustments are included throughout. 

Can I do the nutrition program if I live outside Dallas? 

Yes. Consultations, coaching sessions, lab result reviews, and program adjustments are all available via telehealth. The initial consultation and any in-office lab draws may require an in-person visit, but the ongoing program can run entirely virtually for clients who prefer it. 

How long before I start seeing results? 

Most clients begin noticing meaningful shifts in energy, digestion, and overall wellbeing within the first four to six weeks as dietary changes take effect and deficiencies are addressed. Deeper metabolic and hormonal changes typically become more apparent over three months. This is a program built for lasting results, not a quick fix. 

What aesthetic treatments does JustBE offer? 

JustBE offers Botox and lip filler, both administered exclusively by Dr. Marchuk. These are the only aesthetic services currently available. Every treatment is approached conservatively and with your overall health in mind, not as a standalone procedure sold by volume. 

Do I need to be a wellness client to book Botox or lip filler? 

No. Aesthetic treatments are available as standalone services. That said, Dr. Marchuk may ask questions about your overall health during your consultation because understanding the full picture helps her make better treatment decisions. You are never required to add services you did not come in for. 

How is Botox at JustBE different from a typical medspa? 

Every treatment is administered by Dr. Marchuk directly, every time. No nurses, no rotating injectors, no rushed appointments. The approach here is conservative and anatomy-driven, with the goal of a result that looks like you on a well-rested day, not a visibly treated version of you. 

How long do results from Botox and lip filler last? 

Botox typically lasts three to four months. Lip filler results vary between six and twelve months depending on the product used and how your body processes hyaluronic acid. Clients engaged in a nutrition or wellness program often report longer-lasting aesthetic results due to the overall improvement in their health foundation. 

What should I expect at my first aesthetic appointment? 

Every appointment begins with a consultation. Dr. Marchuk reviews your goals, evaluates your anatomy, and explains exactly what she is recommending and why before anything is administered. There is no pressure and no upselling. If a treatment is not right for you, she will tell you. 

What services are available via telehealth? 

Wellness consultations, nutrition coaching, medical weight loss check-ins, peptide therapy follow-ups, and lab result reviews are all available virtually. Botox and lip filler require an in-person appointment at the Dallas clinic. 

Is telehealth available to clients outside of Texas? 

Currently Dr. Marchuk sees telehealth clients within Texas. If you are located outside the state, reach out directly and we will let you know what options are available to you. 

How do appointments work and how often will I be seen? 

Appointment frequency depends on your program. Medical weight loss clients typically check in monthly or bi-weekly depending on where they are in their protocol. Nutrition and wellness clients schedule based on their individual plan. Dr. Marchuk reviews each case and recommends a cadence that supports your specific goals. 

Can I get a prescription through a telehealth visit? 

In many cases, yes. Prescriptions for weight loss protocols, peptide therapy, and other programs can be issued following a telehealth consultation, provided Dr. Marchuk has your full health history and relevant lab work on file. Medications and peptides are dispensed through licensed pharmacies and shipped directly to your door. 

Does Dr. Marchuk see every client personally? 

Yes, every time. There is no front desk, no nurse, no physician assistant. When you book an appointment at JustBE, in person or virtually, you are speaking directly with Dr. Marchuk. That is true for your first visit and every visit after it.
