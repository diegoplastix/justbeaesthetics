## Frequently Asked Questions

What services are available via telehealth? 

Wellness consultations, nutrition coaching, medical weight loss check-ins, peptide therapy follow-ups, and lab result reviews are all available virtually. Botox and lip filler require an in-person appointment at the Dallas clinic.

Is telehealth available to clients outside of Texas? 

Currently Dr. Marchuk sees telehealth clients within Texas. If you are located outside the state, reach out directly and we will let you know what options are available to you. 

How do appointments work and how often will I be seen? 

Appointment frequency depends on your program. Medical weight loss clients typically check in monthly or bi-weekly depending on where they are in their protocol. Nutrition and wellness clients schedule based on their individual plan. Dr. Marchuk reviews each case and recommends a cadence that supports your specific goals. 

Can I get a prescription through a telehealth visit? 

In many cases, yes. Prescriptions for weight loss protocols, peptide therapy, and other programs can be issued following a telehealth consultation, provided Dr. Marchuk has your full health history and relevant lab work on file. Medications and peptides are dispensed through licensed pharmacies and shipped directly to your door. 

Does Dr. Marchuk see every client personally? 

Yes, every time. There is no front desk, no nurse, no physician assistant. When you book an appointment at JustBE, in person or virtually, you are speaking directly with Dr. Marchuk. That is true for your first visit and every visit after it.
