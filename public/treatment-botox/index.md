## Where Botox Can Help

Botox at JustBE is used conservatively to soften lines, relax overactive facial muscles, and create a more rested appearance without taking away natural expression. Every treatment is personalized by Dr. Marchuk based on your facial movement, goals, and anatomy. Aesthetic care here is meant to enhance, not overpower.

### Forehead Lines

Softens horizontal lines caused by repeated movement across the forehead while keeping the face expressive and natural. 

### Frown Lines

Targets the vertical lines between the brows that can make you look tense, tired, or more severe than you feel. 

### Crow’s Feet

Softens fine lines around the outer eyes with a subtle approach that preserves natural movement and expression. 

### Brow Lift

A precise placement technique that can create a more open, refreshed look without surgery or overcorrection. 

### Bunny Lines

Targets the small lines along the nose that appear with smiling or facial movement, keeping the result soft. 

### Jawline / Chin Softening

In select cases, Botox can help relax facial tension and refine areas of the lower face for a smoother overall look. 

Treatment always begins with a consultation. Dr. Marchuk evaluates your expression patterns, facial balance, and goals before recommending anything. No rushed appointments. No overdone results. Just thoughtful, physician-led care designed to look like you on a well-rested day.

## Frequently Asked Questions

What makes JustBE different from a typical med spa? 

At JustBE, every treatment is administered by Dr. Marchuk directly. No nurses, no rotating injectors, no volume-driven appointments. Botox here is one part of a broader picture of your health, not a standalone service sold by the unit. 

Will Botox make me look unnatural? 

Not when it is done thoughtfully. The goal is to relax targeted muscles while preserving natural expression, so you still look like yourself, just more rested and refreshed. 

How long does Botox last? 

Most clients see results for three to four months. Factors like metabolism, muscle activity, sun exposure, and overall health all influence duration. Clients also engaged in a nutrition or wellness program often report longer-lasting results.
