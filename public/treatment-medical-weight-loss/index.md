## Where The Program Can Help

The medical weight loss program at JustBE is designed to address the physiological factors that most programs never look at. Every protocol is personalized by Dr. Marchuk based on your lab results, your health history, and your goals. 

### Hormonal Imbalance

Hormones regulate metabolism, appetite, fat storage, and energy. When they are off, weight loss feels impossible regardless of what you eat. Lab testing identifies where the imbalance is located. 

### Metabolic Dysfunction

A slow or dysregulated metabolism is not a character flaw. It is a clinical finding. Dr. Marchuk evaluates metabolic markers and builds a protocol that works with your body’s actual function.

### Nutritional Deficiencies

Specific deficiencies in iron, B12, vitamin D, and magnesium directly affect energy, metabolism, and body composition. A CBC panel identifies the gaps so they can be corrected at the source.

### Appetite Regulation

For some clients, GLP-1 based medications or targeted peptide therapy can help regulate appetite and support metabolic function. Prescription decisions made by Dr. Marchuk.

### Chronic Inflammation

Chronic low-grade inflammation is one of the most overlooked barriers to weight loss. Food allergen testing identifies specific triggers so they can be removed from the equation.

### Sustainable Fat Loss

The goal is not the fastest result. It is a result that holds. The program is built to create physiological change that lasts beyond the program itself, not just during it.

Every program begins with a consultation. Dr. Marchuk reviews your full health history, your goals, and your lab results before making any protocol recommendations. No generic plans. No guesswork. Just a clinical approach designed around your specific biology.

## Frequently Asked Questions

What makes JustBE different from other medical weight loss programs? 

At JustBE, the program starts with your labs, your hormones, and your full health history, not a standard protocol handed to every client who walks in. Dr. Marchuk supervises every step directly. No handoffs, no generic plans, no one-size-fits-all approach. 

Are prescription medications part of the program? 

They can be, depending on your assessment. Dr. Marchuk may recommend GLP-1 medications, peptides, compound injections, or vitamin therapy based on your lab results and health history. Nothing is prescribed without a full consultation first. 

How long does the program last? 

Most clients engage with the program for a minimum of three months, which is enough time to complete lab work, implement the protocol, and begin seeing measurable physiological change. Ongoing support is available for clients who want to continue beyond the initial program. 

Is the program available via telehealth? 

Yes. Check-ins, protocol adjustments, and lab result reviews are all available virtually. The initial consultation and any in-office lab draws require an in-person visit. 

Do I need to struggle with a significant amount of weight to qualify? 

No. The program is appropriate for anyone experiencing difficulty managing their weight regardless of how much they are looking to lose. If there is a physiological reason it is not happening, Dr. Marchuk will find it.
