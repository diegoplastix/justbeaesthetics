## Peptide Therapy That 
Works With Your Biology

Peptides are short chains of amino acids that act as messengers in the body. They signal cells to perform specific functions: produce growth hormone, reduce inflammation, improve metabolism, support tissue repair, regulate appetite. As the body ages, natural peptide production declines and these signaling pathways become less efficient.

What separates effective peptide therapy from the noise online is clinical oversight. The accessibility of peptides through unregulated channels has created a market full of unknowns: uncertain purity, incorrect dosing, no assessment, no monitoring. At JustBE, none of that applies. Every protocol starts with a consultation, a review of your labs, and a prescription issued directly by Dr. Marchuk.

Peptides that influence growth hormone secretagogues are typically administered before sleep, supporting deeper sleep cycles and improving the body’s overnight repair and recovery processes.

## Frequently Asked Questions

What are peptides and how are they different from supplements? 

Peptides are prescription compounds, not over-the-counter supplements. They work by signaling specific biological processes at a cellular level. The distinction matters because dosing, sourcing, and clinical assessment are all required for them to work safely and effectively. 

Is peptide therapy safe? 

Yes, when prescribed and monitored by a physician using pharmaceutical-grade compounds. The risk associated with peptide therapy largely comes from unregulated sources and improper dosing. At JustBE, every protocol is clinically supervised from start to finish. 

How do I know which peptides are right for me? 

It starts with a consultation and a review of your labs. Dr. Marchuk evaluates your health history, your goals, and your bloodwork before recommending any specific peptides or dosing protocol. 

How are peptides administered? 

Administration method depends on the specific peptide and protocol. Some are subcutaneous injections, others are oral or nasal. Dr. Marchuk provides full instructions and handles all prescriptions through licensed pharmacies with direct shipping to your door. 

Can peptide therapy be combined with other JustBE programs? 

Yes, and it often works best that way. Peptide therapy at JustBE is designed to complement nutrition coaching, medical weight loss, and hormonal optimization. The synergy between these programs is where the most meaningful results happen.
